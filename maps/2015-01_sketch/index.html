Bike/Ped Advisory Committee

Urban & Rural Sketch Maps

During the December and January meetings of the Bike/Ped Advisory Committee, attendees were split into four groups to think about non-automotive transportation throughout Scott County. Urban-Ped and Urban-Bike focused on Georgetown, while Rural-Ped and Rural-Bike concentrated on Sadieville, Stamping Ground, and the rest of Scott County.

Each group was given a large map of their assigned area and a box of markers. Members were encouraged to markup the map as a way of brainstorming the various observations and desires for their mode of transport in their assigned area.

You can view the results* of this exercise by scrolling down.

*Scroll-wheel zooming is disabled on the maps. Use the zoom buttons (+/-) to zoom in. Alternatively, you can double-click to zoom in on a location or Shift + double-click to zoom out.
